# Stanvault Market Positioning

## Market
Stanvault is built for premium independent artists, boutique labels, and management teams with high-intent fan bases and strong direct monetization potential.

This is not a mass-volume creator tool. It is relationship infrastructure for teams optimizing for fan quality, retention, and lifetime value.

## Psychographic Profile
Core users are:
- Brand-conscious and reputation-protective.
- Focused on depth of fan relationship over follower count.
- Willing to invest in premium systems and operations.
- Measuring outcomes by conversion, retention, and repeat spend.

They are building evergreen fan businesses, not short-term engagement spikes.

## Who This Is For
- Artists with affluent and loyal superfans.
- Managers building durable fan revenue engines.
- Teams that want precision segmentation and premium communication workflows.
- Operators who value insight-led growth and long-term fan equity.

## Who This Is Not For
- Creators focused only on viral reach and vanity metrics.
- Teams that optimize for low-intent volume at all costs.
- Buyers unwilling to invest in premium fan infrastructure.
- Workflows that treat fans as disposable traffic.

## Product Packaging Strategy
Use Stanvault as the intelligence layer and Emissar as the delivery layer.

- Stanvault core: segmentation, scoring, dashboard, campaign planning.
- Emissar: live campaign execution, advanced personalization, orchestration.

Recommended structure:
- Base tier includes Stanvault core plus dry-run campaign workflows.
- Higher tiers unlock live Emissar delivery and higher send limits.

## Pricing Framework (Luxury Positioning)
- Private Circle — $299/mo  
Fit: early high-intent artists.

- Patron Growth — $899/mo  
Fit: growing premium brands running live fan campaigns.

- Sovereign — $2,500+/mo  
Fit: top-tier teams requiring high-touch support and custom automation.

## Strategic Positioning Statement
Stanvault is for artists who treat superfans as long-term assets.
It helps premium creators own fan intelligence and activate it through luxury-grade communication, without sacrificing brand integrity.

## ORYX Alignment (Markets, Currencies, Moats)
- Keep Stanvault and Oryx aligned on priority launch markets: Nigeria, South Africa, Kenya, Ghana, Uganda.
- Keep market-facing currency defaults aligned: `NGN`, `ZAR`, `KES`, `GHS`, `UGX` (plus `USD`, `GBP`, `EUR` for global buyers).
- Use one strategic posture across both products: go deep in fewer high-conviction markets before broad expansion.

Blue Ocean moat alignment:
- Hyperlocal trust moat: city and culture-specific fan operations outperform generic global tooling.
- Economic-context moat: conviction and value signals should reflect local purchasing context, not only raw spend.
- System moat: Stanvault (intelligence) + Emissar (activation) + Oryx market rails compounds advantage over point tools.
