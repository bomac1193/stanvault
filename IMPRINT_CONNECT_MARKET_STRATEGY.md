# Imprint Connect Market Strategy

## Thesis

Imprint should not try to expose every possible API as a generic connect button.

The product split is:

- Imprint: audience sources, fan identity, consent, campaigns, direct reach.
- Oryx: payments, conviction, referrals, and market rails.

That split keeps the artist-facing surface simple while preserving the real moat in Oryx.

## What Goes In Imprint

### Audience Sources

- Spotify
- YouTube
- Discord
- Email List
- Oryx
- Instagram later
- TikTok later
- Apple Music later
- Bandcamp when access/import is ready
- Boomplay only as partner/manual import

### Reach Channels

- WhatsApp
- SMS
- LINE
- Kakao

These are not just notifications. They are the actual ownership layer in phone-first and fan-club-heavy markets.

## What Stays In Oryx

- Paystack
- Flutterwave
- M-Pesa
- MTN MoMo
- payment verification
- referral attribution
- conviction events

Do not surface these as standalone artist-facing connect buttons in Imprint.

## Market Order

### Africa

Ship first.

Why:

- strongest fit with Oryx conviction rails
- phone-first fan behavior
- WhatsApp and SMS are strategically more valuable than another DSP
- Afrohouse, Afrobeats, Amapiano, and manager-led teams already map to the product

Priority stack:

- YouTube
- Spotify
- Email List
- Oryx
- WhatsApp
- SMS

### Europe

Expand next through diaspora artist-managers and community-led collectives.

Priority stack:

- YouTube
- Spotify
- Email List
- Discord
- WhatsApp

### America

Expand after Europe through diaspora teams and direct-to-fan operators who already capture owned contact points.

Priority stack:

- YouTube
- Spotify
- Email List
- Discord
- WhatsApp
- SMS

### Asia

Do not broad-launch yet.

Go selectively after:

- Kakao
- LINE
- fan-club identity primitives
- stronger private-community consent UX

Best-fit segments:

- K-pop micro labels
- fan-club leads
- street-team operators

## CTA Direction

The flagship CTA should not be "stream now".

The strongest pattern is:

- claim identity
- complete one proof-backed action
- unlock access

Working shape:

`Claim your role in this release. Bring proof or bring one real listener and unlock the private drop.`

## Why This Matters

Another analytics connector does not create ownership.

Direct channels plus conviction rails do.
