# ICP Beta Stress Test

## Scope

Admin preview now includes a fixed in-memory market simulation:

- 30 ICP profiles
- 10 non-customers
- 10 haters

This is a pressure test for market fit, blind spots, and rollout order. It does not write to the database.

## Result

### Core Audience

The strongest audience is:

- African music operators
- Afrohouse, Amapiano, Afrobeats, and manager-led teams
- people who already have direct community channels
- people who can feed conviction through Oryx or similar proof-backed behavior

### Expand Next

- Europe through diaspora artist-managers
- America through diaspora teams and direct-to-fan operators

### Selective Only

- Asia, until LINE, Kakao, and fan-club identity are real product primitives

## Why Africa Wins

- highest concentration of direct community behavior
- strongest overlap with Oryx conviction rails
- phone-first messaging matters more than another analytics API
- cultural behavior already matches the ownership thesis

## Top Blind Spots Exposed

- no WhatsApp-native ownership flow yet
- SMS exists conceptually but still needs full delivery rollout
- Boomplay and regional DSP visibility are still thin
- privacy and anti-spam objections need stronger consent framing
- Asia needs fan-club messaging and identity before a broad push

## Product Implications

### Imprint

Own:

- audience-source connections
- consent
- segmentation
- campaigns
- direct messaging channels

### Oryx

Own:

- payments
- referrals
- mobile-money verification
- conviction and revenue rails

## Recommended Build Order

1. WhatsApp and SMS as campaign channels
2. Deepen YouTube and Discord
3. Keep Oryx as the conviction engine
4. Add Bandcamp only when access/import is ready
5. Handle Boomplay through partner/manual import
6. Add LINE and Kakao before trying to broad-launch Asia

## How To Use The Simulation

- Switch admin preview to `Beta Simulation`
- View `/dashboard` for region fit, rollout order, blind spots, and API priorities
- View `/fans` to inspect the simulated profiles directly

In this mode, tier labels represent product-fit strength:

- `SUPERFAN` = core now
- `DEDICATED` = expand next
- `ENGAGED` = selective
- `CASUAL` = not now
